    About
    Me

A local of Asheville, North Carolina, Daniel comes from a strong technical and design background. After studying film in school, Daniel went on to live in South Korea, where his design and visual composition skills flourished. After four years of living in Asia, Daniel returned to Asheville and spent over two years representing Asheville's local tourism industry, acting as a technical liaison between the sales department and the greater organization.

Daniel's web applications combine a balance of visually-appeasing content and a strong sense functionality. Design and user experience are essential, and each of his applications share a story that will not only leave clients satisfied, but also connected. Daniel's favorite hobbies include photography, rollerblading, eating Korean food, and exploring the Blue Ridge Mountains.
