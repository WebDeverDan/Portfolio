    About
    Me

I am a full stack web developer with a front-end focus, and have a background in visual media production, business, and client relations. Recently earning a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from UNC-Chapel Hill, and as a Mass Communications graduate of UNC-Asheville, I bring my strong design background, knack for user experience, and understanding of web application development to the table. Web development skills include HTML, JavaScript, JQuery, CSS, and navigation of CRM platforms, along with design skills including proficiency in Adobe and Apple video and photo editing software, various video capturing systems, and an equally-strong knowledge of both the Spanish and Korean languages.

My goals for each completed application consist of effective functionality, a clean and modern interface, and seamless ease of use. For example, in Summer of 2021, I worked alongside a team of 3 other web developers to create a video game themed platform which enabled users to find a random game by desired platform. My attention to detail and design enabled the site to have a modern feel along with an easy to use interface. I am thrilled to use my web and design skill set to enhance current and future web experiences for clients and users alike.
